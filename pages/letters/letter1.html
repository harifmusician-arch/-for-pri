❤️ Read When You Miss Me

Hi Pri, If you're reading this, I'm guessing one of two things happened. Either you miss me. Or you're snooping through everything on this website. Honestly, both are very on brand. I know distance can be annoying sometimes. There are days when a text isn't the same as a conversation, and a conversation isn't the same as being there. I feel that too. But I want you to remember something. The distance between two people is measured in kilometers. Missing someone is measured in how often they cross your mind. And somehow, you manage to do that a lot. More than I'd probably admit out loud. So if today feels a little lonely, or if you wish I was sitting beside you instead of existing through a screen, just remember this: You are thought about more often than you know. You are cared about more deeply than you realize. And no amount of distance can change either of those things. Also, while you're here: Drink some water. Eat something. And stop overthinking whatever you're overthinking. Yes, I know you're probably overthinking something. I hope by the time you finish reading this, you smile at least once. And if not, that's okay too. I'll settle for knowing you reached this page. Missing someone is proof that they matter. So thank you for missing me. Because I'd be lying if I said I never miss you too. — Hari ❤️
