Read When You Feel Alone ❤️

Hi Pri, I don't know what kind of day you're having right now. Maybe it's one of those days where everything feels a little quieter than usual. Maybe everyone is busy. Maybe nobody seems to understand what's going on in your head. Or maybe you're surrounded by people and somehow still feel alone. Whatever it is, I want you to know something. You are not nearly as alone as you think you are. I know loneliness has a way of convincing us otherwise. It sits beside us and whispers things that aren't true. It tells us nobody notices. Nobody cares. Nobody understands. But loneliness is a terrible storyteller. Because the truth is that there are people who care about you. People whose days are better because you're in them. People who smile when your name appears on their screen. People who would be sad if you weren't around. And whether you realize it or not, you're one of those people for me. You have occupied more space in my thoughts than you probably know. You've made ordinary days feel less ordinary. You've made me laugh when I didn't expect to. You've given me memories that I know I'll keep for a very long time. So if today feels heavy, don't try to carry all of it by yourself. You don't have to be strong every second. You don't have to have everything figured out. You don't have to pretend you're okay if you're not. Take a breath. Get some rest. Listen to a song you love. Look at the sky for a minute. And remember that somewhere out there is a person who genuinely hopes you're doing okay. Me. And before you leave this letter, here's one last thing: The world is better with you in it. I hope someday you believe that as much as I do. — Hari ❤️
