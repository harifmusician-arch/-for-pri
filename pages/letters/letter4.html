Happy Birthday, Pri ❤️

I wanted to get you a birthday gift, but since I can't exactly send anything without risking a heart attack from your mum 😭, I thought I'd give you something a little more honest instead. You know what's funny? People always say that when you meet someone special, you'll just know. I never believed that. I thought feelings were something that grew slowly, and maybe they are. But looking back, I think a part of me knew long before I did. Why else would I save our first conversations, the first jokes, the first screenshots, the first little moments when we were practically strangers? I didn't know where any of this was going. I just knew I didn't want to lose it. This time last year, I was heartbroken in Nepal, convinced life had already played its joke on me. I had no idea that a few months later I'd meet someone who would quietly become one of the most important people in my life. Somehow, all my songs sound like they're about you now. All my poems end up finding their way back to you. Every road I thought was leading somewhere else somehow led here. The biggest gift you've given me isn't love, or affection, or even happiness. It's peace. For most of my life, affection felt like something that had to be earned. With you, it never felt that way. You make me feel seen, heard, understood, and safe. Safe enough to be myself. Safe enough to let my guard down. Safe enough that, somehow, talking to you is one of the few things that can actually make me sleepy 😭. And if there's one thing I want you to know on your birthday, it's this: thank you. Thank you for choosing to come back every day. Thank you for the conversations, the laughter, the late nights, the music, the memories, and all the little moments that probably seemed ordinary at the time but ended up meaning the world to me. I don't know what the future looks like. I don't know how many miles will sit between us or how long we'll have to wait. But I do know this: if I had to take a twenty-hour flight just to see you for a few seconds, I'd do it without thinking twice. Because meeting you has been one of the best things that has ever happened to me. Happy Birthday, Pri. ❤️ And fair warning... your future boyfriend is a little poetic.
