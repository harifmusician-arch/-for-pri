Things I Never Said ❤️

Hi Pri, There are probably a hundred things I've thought about saying to you and never did. Some because I was nervous. Some because the moment passed. Some because certain feelings are harder to say out loud than they are to write. So here are a few of them. I never told you how often I reread our conversations. I never told you how many screenshots ended up in my gallery because I didn't want to forget a moment that made me smile. I never told you how many times I saw something during my day and immediately thought, "I need to tell Pri about this." I never told you that sometimes your name appearing on my screen was enough to improve my mood. I never told you that some of my favorite memories of this year involve nothing extraordinary. Just conversations. Just messages. Just you being there. I never told you how many songs became associated with you without your knowledge. How certain lyrics suddenly sounded different because they reminded me of you. How some nights felt shorter because I was talking to you. How some days felt easier because I knew I would. I never told you how much I admire the little things about you. Not the obvious things. The small things. The things you probably don't even notice about yourself. The way you care. The way you think. The way you make people feel comfortable. The way you can make me laugh when I'm trying very hard not to. I never told you how grateful I am that our paths crossed at all. Because honestly, they didn't have to. A different decision. A different day. A different conversation. And maybe we never meet. But somehow we did. And I'm thankful for that. Most of all... I never properly told you this. Not in a way that felt complete. Not in a way that captured everything I meant. So I'll say it here. I love you. Not because you're perfect. Not because every day is easy. Not because we never disagree. I love you because somewhere along the way you became important to me. More important than I expected. More important than I planned for. You became part of my days. Part of my thoughts. Part of my favorite memories. And whether this letter is read today, next month, or years from now, that truth remains the same. I love you. And I think a part of me always will. — Hari ❤️
